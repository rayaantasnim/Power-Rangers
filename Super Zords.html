Dragon Zord

The Dragon Zord is the supreme aerial Guardian Zord of the IDAI Power Rangers, designed for rapid deployment, long-range operations, and unmatched flight performance. Stationed within a specialized launch hangar beneath the IDAI Power Rangers Headquarters, it remains ready for immediate activation by the Cragnon Command Nexus. When deployed, the headquarters' massive armored roof automatically opens, allowing the Dragon Zord to soar into the sky and respond swiftly to missions across Earth and beyond.

Powered by an advanced Proxima Centauri quantum energy core, the Dragon Zord features intelligent flight systems, adaptive armor, powerful energy wings, and seamless communication with the Cragnon Super Quantum Computer. It serves as the command aerial platform of the ranger fleet and can synchronize with the six Guardian Zords to form the legendary Dragon Mega-Zord. As a symbol of unity, innovation, and guardianship, the Dragon Zord stands ready to protect humanity whenever its strength is called upon.
